High-Impact Visualizations:
Attrition By Job Role: Identifies the roles with the highest number of departures, with Laboratory Technician being the most impacted (62 departures).

Attrition By Salary: Shows that the "Upto 5K" salary band accounts for the overwhelming majority of attrition (163 departures).

Attrition By Years in Company: Reveals a critical point of departure for employees who have served less than 2 years, with the highest peak at the first year (59 departures).

Attrition By Education: Highlights that employees with a Life Sciences education account for the highest percentage of attrition (38%).
